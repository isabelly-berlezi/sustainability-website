Our Mission

Our mission is to raise awareness about sustainability and encourage people to make meaningful changes. As ateam, we chose this topic beacuse we believe in protecting Our planet for future genrations.

Our sustainability Goal

Our chosen goal is Sustainable Development Goal 13: Climate Action. This goal focuses on reducing greenhouse gas emissions, promoting renewable energy, and encouraging people to protect the environment for future generations.
