There are endless possibilities to help out our planet and future generations with some basic tasks that can greatly reduce pollution caused by rubbish. The most common saying "Reduce, Reuse, Recycle" is a great place to start. Properly recycling your waste—things like separating plastic, glass, and metal—is all recycled separately, diverting it from landfills.
Organizations like TeamSeas is a massive fundraiser to help reduce plastic waste left in the seas. Their main goal is to remove 30 million pounds of ocean plastic by raising $30 million. Small donations towards fundraisers like TeamSeas are a great way to contribute.
